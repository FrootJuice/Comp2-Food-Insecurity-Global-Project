Campus Resources

Resources available to Northwest Missouri State University students experiencing food insecurity.

Bearcat Food Pantry

The on-campus food pantry provides free non-perishable food items to Northwest students. Located in room 2200 of the Student Union. No appointment needed, but bring your Bearcat ID card and check in with Student Affairs.

Visit Bearcat Food Pantry

Community Resources

Local Maryville resources including food pantries, SNAP benefits, and community assistance programs.

SNAP Benefits (Food Stamps)

The Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program provides food benefits to low-income individuals and families in Missouri. Check eligibility requirements and apply online through the Missouri Department of Social Services.

Apply for SNAP

Maryville Ministry Center Food Pantry

Community food pantry serving Nodaway County residents. Open Monday, Wednesday, and Friday from 1:30 PM to 3:30 PM. Must meet federal income guidelines and live in Nodaway County.
